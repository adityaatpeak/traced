How It Works

From Alert to Resolution
in One Slack Thread

An alert fires. Traced takes it from there — investigating, correlating, and recommending a fix before your team finishes triaging.

1

Alert Triggers Investigation

PagerDuty, Alertmanager, or any custom webhook fires. Traced ingests the alert, deduplicates it, and matches it against built-in runbooks for common incident patterns.

2

Agents Investigate in Parallel

Specialist AI agents fan out simultaneously — querying Prometheus metrics, searching Elasticsearch logs, inspecting Kubernetes state, and checking recent deployments.

3

Root Cause Synthesized

Findings are correlated across all sources. Claude reasons through the evidence, ranks hypotheses by confidence, and builds a timeline of events leading to the incident.

4

Fix Suggested with Evidence

A specific remediation is proposed in Slack with blast radius assessment, safety tier classification, and the full evidence chain supporting the recommendation.

5

Approve, Execute, Verify

One click in Slack. The fix is executed, verified against live metrics, and a resolution summary is posted to the thread. The full incident is stored for future learning.

Native webhooks for PagerDuty, Alertmanager, and a generic endpoint for any monitoring tool. Built-in deduplication prevents alert storms.

Runbook Engine

Pre-built runbooks for OOM kills, high latency, CrashLoopBackOff, disk pressure, certificate expiry, and connection exhaustion. Add your own in YAML.

Incident Dashboard

Web UI for browsing investigations, viewing agent findings, and tracking remediation status. Filter by severity, status, or affected service.

RBAC & Approvals

Role-based access with viewer, responder, and admin tiers. Map Slack users to roles and control who can approve each action level.

⚙

Parallel Agents

Observability, Kubernetes, and change detection agents run simultaneously. Root cause in minutes, not the hours it takes to check each dashboard.

🗃

Incident Memory

Every investigation is stored with full context — findings, actions taken, verification results. Build institutional knowledge that persists across team changes.

Why Traced

Built Different from Existing AIOps

Most AIOps tools are too shallow, too opaque, or force your sensitive data out of your environment. We built something better.

01

Your Data Stays Inside Your Walls

The collector runs in your cluster. Sensitive data is filtered before it ever leaves. Only summarized investigation context reaches the AI. Built for teams that take compliance seriously.

02

Transparent Agents, Not a Black Box

Specialist agents for observability, Kubernetes, and change detection — each shows its work with full evidence chains, transparent reasoning, and auditable decisions.

03

Slack-Native from Day One

Investigations happen in Slack threads with Block Kit interactions. Approve fixes, view timelines, follow progress in real time. Zero new dashboards to learn.

04

Safety-First Remediation

Every action is classified by risk — from read-only to high-risk. Dangerous operations are never automated. Role-based approvals ensure the right people sign off.
