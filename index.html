Why Traced

Built different from existing AIOps

Existing AIOps tools are either too shallow, too opaque, or force your data out of your environment. We built something better.

01

Data Stays Internal

Your production data — logs, metrics, traces — never leaves your environment. Only summarized investigation context goes to the LLM. Critical for regulated industries.

02

Multi-Agent, Not Monolith

Specialist agents you can extend, swap, or add to. Not a black box — an open architecture your team can customize for your stack.

03

Slack-Native UX

Zero new dashboards. Investigations happen where your team already communicates. Tag @traced and follow along in the thread.

04

Parallel Investigation

Specialist agents run simultaneously — metrics, logs, K8s state, and change history are queried at once, not sequentially. Target: root cause in under 10 minutes.
